Biography

Fear, and Loathing in Las Vegas (sometimes abbreviated as FALILV) are a Japanese band from Kobe City, founded in 2008.

Their three demo EPs have continued to approach immediate sold outs. Following them up with the highly acclaimed 1st full album “Dance&Scream” in October 2010, still continuing to be a massive hit today.

With an average age of 19, they have showed drastic success with their new generation talents. Unlike typical bands they have been taking in and blending various genres such as emo/screamo/metal/ and dance tunes. Stuffed with numerous genres and originality, it goes without saying that their music is what’s called “chaos”.

Mixed with a dynamic melody of clean vocals and destructive shouts, the combination of this unique twin vocal style has shown a dramatic approach to the audiences. Their hot and steamy live performances, at all times exceeding boiling point have been burning the dancing crowd; flooded with live offers all over Japan.

In 2011, the band continued to explore new sounds with their mini album “NEXTREME”, which gave the band their first top 10 on the official Japanese CD sales chart, still showing an explosive hit today.

In January 2012, their first run limited edition EP “Just Awake” has sold out in no time. Later that same year in August, they have released their 2nd full album “All That We Have Now”. As titled, they have put in and expressed “all that they have now”; peaked at number 4 in the official Japanese chart right after their release.

They have played in 37 domestic cities on their release tour; performing all 37 sold out shows. Their first tour in 2013, Zepp tour “Short Tour 2013″, was not surprising to be rapidly marked as sold out. Their still accelerating powerful performances will at no doubt fascinate more and more audiences in 2013.
